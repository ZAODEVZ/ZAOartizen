# Plan 3: Run the ZAO Fund for Emerging Culture

Zaal curates it. $10k Season 6, 32 projects in competition, 15 in curation (as of the Jun 11 snapshot;
numbers move). The fund is the reciprocity engine behind Plans 1 and 2: it is what makes every ask on the
platform a trade instead of a favor. Unused balance carries into Season 7 (Rene-confirmed).

## The three jobs

### 1. Make it a community, not a list (feeds Plan 1)
- Add the engagement rule to the fund page (directors can edit requirements at will, Rene-confirmed):
  "Active collaboration is required, not optional. Curated projects are expected to connect: reach out,
  meet the curator, take part. Projects that do not engage by season end may be removed to make room for
  creators who will."
- Run the end-of-season outreach: 8-12 real conversations, warm six first (Arun, Telamon, Moses, Gneric,
  KOSBAAR, Jose - verify). Channel: Artizen comments, Farcaster DM, X DM. NOT a 32-blast in the final week.
- Every meet ends with a give (boost, feature, workshop slot, intro).

### 2. Curation pipeline (feeds Plan 2's reciprocity)
- Standing invitation everywhere: "if your project bridges music, tech, and community, submit and DM me."
- Committed asks in flight: MWA (James), We're Loud Fest (Pete), Bonfires-ecosystem projects.
- Review submissions weekly (Thursday, with the drive turnover). Accept on fit with the fund's verbatim
  eligibility (creator-owned, emerging tech as infrastructure not gimmick, builds in public, fair-comp,
  culminates in a real-world activation).
- Re-curation is allowed (directors can drop projects that no longer fit) - the engagement rule is the
  published basis, applied at season boundaries, never mid-drive.

### 3. Fill the pool (the flywheel)
- Fund prize rolls into next drive's match pool: rank the fund by getting ZAO crew to BOOST THE FUND, not
  only the projects. Free, and it compounds for all 32.
- Sponsorships: the sponsor rail copy exists (kit/sponsor-onepager.md, /sponsor page). Top sponsor is
  featured as Presenting Sponsor on the fund. Web3 targets in kit/web3-sponsor-targets.md.
- Zaal's own projects sitting in the fund make it less dormant by existing (both are submitted to it).
