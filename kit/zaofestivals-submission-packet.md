Everything to put the ZAO Festivals project live on Artizen. Fields match the official Playbook
(play.artizen.fund / ZAOOS doc 887). Submit at **artizen.fund/submit** (connect wallet first).

## 3. Artifact brief (the one thing only you can make)

Spec (hard rules): **square 1:1, >=1000px** image (.jpg/.png) OR **<=45s .mp4 loop** (or .gif >=500px),
**NO text, logos, or graphic overlays**. It is the soul of the project, not a flyer. Video/GIF outperform static.

Three options - pick one:
1. A square **video loop of a past ZAO live set** - crowd + artist energy, no captions.
2. A square **shot/illustration of the Franklin St Parklet** (Ellsworth) staged for the festival.
3. An abstract **"ZAO Festivals" motion piece** - the vibe as a looping gif.

Fastest path: pull a clean 10-20s clip from existing ZAO event footage, crop square, strip any text.
