# Season 6 -> Season 7 transition checklist

Season 6 closed July 9, 2026 (creators raised $8.3M platform-wide, corrected figure). Season 7 is live now,
running through roughly Dec 2026/Jan 2027. Full source: research/851-artizen-season6-close-season7-launch.
This file is the fast, copy-paste-ready to-do list - the research doc has the receipts.

## Do this now

- [ ] **Claim Season 6 payouts.** Log into grow.artizen.fund with the Artizen account email, open Payouts,
      complete the form (name, country, tax form, USD-to-bank or USDC-to-wallet) for every ZAO-fund-backed
      project. Not first-come-first-served - no rush, but don't skip it. Takes a few weeks to clear after
      submission (the team reconciles every project's numbers before sending real money).
- [ ] **Refresh the Season 7 Artifact** for BCZ Strategies + ZAO Festivals (already planned in
      kit/season7-artifact-plan.md). No need to resubmit either project or re-apply to any fund it's
      already curated in - curation carries over automatically. Only the Artifact itself needs a new
      upload, square/1:1, no text.
- [ ] **Attend a Funders Forum** (Mondays, 11am PT, luma.com/funders-forum-N). This is where Season 7
      curation rules, match caps, and the "association" definition are being decided WITH fund directors
      live. Attending also pays: 25,000 Boost Points just for showing up, plus a recording link after.
- [ ] **Follow up on the 20% fund-director compensation.** Confirmed verbally by Venus/René in the Telegram
      (20% of sponsor dollars raised for the fund, match/prize excluded, payable as contractor payment /
      nonprofit donation / crypto / check) but not yet found in any official written doc - ask for it in
      writing at the next Funders Forum before counting on it.
- [ ] **Tell ZAO creators and curators: Boost Points never expire.** Only the weekly leaderboard resets,
      not the point balance. (Venus told the community the opposite for a few days in early July - that was
      wrong and got corrected. If anyone's still operating on "spend before the drive closes," clear it up.)
