## Artizen deep dive (what is verified vs not)

Ground truth here is the LIVE project page (Zaal's screenshot, 2026-07-03) plus doc 843 (grounded in Rene
Pinnell's June 2026 newsletter). A research agent claimed "Season 6 ended June 18, Season 7 is live" - that is

**Verified / high confidence:**
- We are in the Season 6 FINALE fund drive. It ends in about 5 days (so ~July 8 to 9). Season 6 was extended
  once to July 9 and will not move again.
- Two phases: Curation (community voting, top ~30 percent by votes advance) then Competition (Artifact sales +
  match). $1 contributed = 10 votes; a completed profile = 100 votes.
- Scoring on a project page: RANK = leaderboard position (driven by sales). SCORE = total raised (sales +
  match). RAISED = what artifact sales brought in. AVAILABLE = the match still claimable for this project (the
  fund pool, ~90 percent, split across curated projects). BOOST = a multiplier on the match. PRIZE = the top
  slice (about 10 percent of the fund) to the top-ranked project.
- The multi-fund multiplier is real: a $1 artifact sale pulls a $1 match from EACH fund the project is curated
  into. In 3 funds, one sale can move the score by roughly $3. This is why submitting to multiple funds is the
  single biggest lever.
- BCZ Strategies right now: Rank #939, Score 1.00, $10 raised, $2,000 available match, no fund submissions yet.
  So the entire $2,000 is unclaimed and the clock is ~5 days.
