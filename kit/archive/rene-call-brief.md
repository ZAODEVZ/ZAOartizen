# René Pinnell call brief

For Zaal's 15-min call with René (Artizen founder). Goal: greenlight a second ZAO fund + settle
the open mechanics. Send the DM first (outreach-drafts.md #1) to book it.

## One-line framing

The ZAO is already a fund director on Artizen with a real 188-member community that buys and boosts.
We want to go from "runs a fund" to "flagship community" - two funds, the whole portfolio on, and
we bring the crowd.

## The 3 asks (decisions to get)

1. **Two funds?** Can one org/director run the ZAO Fund for Emerging Culture AND a new ZAO Festivals
   Fund? Any governance or capacity guideline?
2. **Self-curation?** Can a fund director curate their own community's projects (e.g. ZAOstock) into
   their own fund - or only into other funds?
3. **Second seed?** Does a second fund get its own up-to-$50K seed, or is the $1M-for-Community-Funds
   program one-per-organization?

## What we bring (talking points)

- 188-member, 4-year, Base/Farcaster music + culture community - a built-in buyer + voter base most
  funds lack. (Console's fund bar was 50 members; we're 4x.)
- A portfolio, not one project: WaveWarZ, SongJam, ZAOstock, Thy Revolution, the ZABAL Games cohort.
- Already a director (ZAO Fund for Emerging Culture, 32 projects) - past the "can you run a fund" question.
- On-narrative: artist-first, regen/ReFi-adjacent, network-state-curious - fits the Artizen / DWeb / Edge City world.

## The Festivals Fund pitch (the white space)

Greenpill owns regenerative gatherings, Terminus owns network states, Hubs owns permanent spaces -
nobody owns music + artist-led IRL festivals. The ZAO Festivals Fund fills that lane and pulls in
festival organizers who want a fund that gets live culture. (Full proposal: kit/fund-proposal.md.)

## What we want from Artizen

- Approval + seed for the ZAO Festivals Fund.
- A nod on self-curation so we can put our own events in our funds.
- Warm intro path to event/music sponsors for the fund's match pool.

## Soft asks (if it flows)

- A ZAO music act on Artizen LIVE.
- A path to the DWeb Camp Artizen Village (Berlin, Jul 8-12).
