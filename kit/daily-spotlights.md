# ZAO Fund - Daily Project Spotlight Series

A daily post spotlighting one project in the ZAO Fund for Emerging Culture on Artizen.
Goal: get each creator seen, drive their artifact sales (which unlocks ZAO Fund match
straight to them), and build the ZAO Fund's reputation as a fund that shows up for its people.

Source roster: research/business/843-zao-fund-artizen-roster-june2026 (snapshot 2026-06-12).
Fund link: https://artizen.thezao.com/

---

## The recurring mechanic (keep this accurate)

Supporting a project = collecting their $10 Artifact. Because they are curated into the ZAO
Fund, every $1 they raise unlocks $1 of matching from the fund, paid to them. So "support the
project" and "support the ZAO Fund" are the same action - the fund is the multiplier.

## Standard CTA line (reuse / vary lightly)

> [Project] is part of the ZAO Fund for Emerging Culture on Artizen. One of the best ways to
> back them: collect their artifact - it unlocks matching from our fund, straight to the creator.
> Support them, support the fund: artizen.thezao.com

## Format

- 3-5 lines. Plain, warm, specific. No emojis, no em dashes, no hype words.
- Line 1: who + what they make (the hook).
- Line 2-3: why it matters / what's interesting.
- Line 4: the CTA (the mechanic above, trimmed to fit).
- Add the direct project link when you grab it from the fund page ("See project").
- Tag the creator's handle if you have it.
