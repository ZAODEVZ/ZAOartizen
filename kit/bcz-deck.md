For the Artizen "pitch deck" upload. Build in Canva, Slides, or Figma. Rule: big beautiful image per slide,
minimal text, one idea each. Tell the story, do not write an essay. Built from the real material.

- Visual: Zaal building (hands on the keyboard, screens glowing) or a clean BCZ mark.
- Text: "BetterCallZaal Strategies" / "The $10 Hour."

- Text: "Streaming pays artists $0.003 a play. Platforms own the audience and the data. The tools to fix it
