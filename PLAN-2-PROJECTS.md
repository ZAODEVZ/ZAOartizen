## Standing loop (every Thursday drive)

1. **Submit + DM.** Work down kit/fund-targets-and-directors.md until both projects sit in 5+ funds each.
   Submit on the fund page, THEN DM the director (platform notifies nobody). No reply is not a rejection.
2. **Crew push = collects AND boosts together.** Boost Score is multiplicative: (sales + match) x boosts.
   Either alone caps out. The ask to the crew: buy one $10 artifact AND click your boosts through.
3. **Early beats late.** Available match depletes in real time within each drive. Coordinate pushes for the
   first day of a drive, not the last.
4. **Update cadence: 2-3 posts a week per project** during any active push. Day-1 thank-you is drafted.
   Thank backers by name (supporter email download button once the bug is fixed).
5. **Boost the ZAO Fund itself.** The fund's weekly prize rolls into its next-drive match pool, which
   compounds for every ZAO project in it.
