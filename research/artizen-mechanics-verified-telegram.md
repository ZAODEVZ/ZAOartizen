## Drive + scoring mechanics (sharper than we had)

- **Drives are weekly, Thursday to Thursday.** One always live, no gaps. Can shift around season close (July 9).
- **Rank and boost-point balances RESET each drive.** Season-cumulative boosts/votes are tracked separately
  and count toward the end-of-season prize at the July 9 close. The final-drive algorithm was "still
  finalizing" per Rene on 2026-07-03; expect scoring wobble in the finale.
- **Boost Score = (total sales + total match unlocked) x total boost points received / 100. MULTIPLICATIVE.**
  Huge sales with few boosts stays capped; tons of boosts with no sales stays capped. Both floor at 1.
  Winning combo = sales AND community heat together.
- **Prize curve:** #1 largest, #2 = 50% of #1, #3 = 50% of #2, then a flatter curve; everyone ranked earns
  something. Raised (sales + unlocked match) is LOCKED once made; Prize fluctuates until the Thursday close.
- **Available match depletes in REAL TIME.** It is a shared pool per fund. Act early in a drive; a hot fund
  can run dry. Available = sum over funds curating you of remaining pool + Endowment per-project allocation,
  scaled by the drive's Match Multiple (same multiple for everyone in a drive).
- **Joining a new fund mid-drive** can start applying during that drive (curation status is computed live at
  drive start/end), but applying early positions strongest for the next drive.
- **Sponsorships vs sales:** sales = artifact buys on a project; sponsorships = money into a FUND's match
  pool. "Presented By" on a project = top sponsors of the funds backing it, not direct buyers.
- **Fund flywheel:** a fund's weekly prize does NOT pay out as cash; it rolls into that fund's match pool for
  the next drive. So boosting/sponsoring the ZAO Fund compounds match for every project it curates.
  Creators boosting their own curating fund is rational and by design.

## Fund-fit discovery (Venus's own guidance)

- There is NO tag-based fund search. The index is scroll-and-read; matching is organic by design.
- Best practice per Venus: read a fund's lineup (who it already curated) over its description, then
  **DM the fund director directly** with a one-line framed fit. "A nudge from you is worth far more than
  waiting" - the platform does not notify either way on submissions.
- No response to a submission does NOT mean rejected; curation is rolling all season. But a fund can be out
  of match, in which case new curations get no match even if accepted.
- **Venus offers a matchmaking pass**: give it a project link + core themes and it maps funds whose lens
  fits. USE THIS for both ZAO projects (messages drafted in the kit).
