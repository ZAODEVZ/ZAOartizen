# ZAO Festivals - 1-page brand book

The single reference so the team stays consistent. Built from the learnings log (2026-06-28). Keep it to a page.

## What ZAO Festivals is (the line)
A series of free, artist-built, community-owned music festivals by The ZAO. Free to attend, artists paid
fairly, owned by the crowd that funds it. A festival as a protocol, not a product.

## The naming system (our strongest asset)
Every festival is **ZAO-[festival name]**. Lean into it - it is ownable and infinitely extensible.
- ZAO-PALOOZA (NYC, Apr 2024) - ZAO-CHELLA (Miami, Dec 2024) - ZAOville (DMV, 2026) - ZAOstock (Ellsworth, Oct 3 2026)
- Rule: new chapter = ZAO + a play on a known festival name. Hyphenate the "ZAO-X" ones (ZAO-PALOOZA), close up
  the suffix ones (ZAOstock, ZAOville). Always the exact casing above.

## Color
- Navy `#0a1628` (base / shadow)
- Gold `#f5a623` (accent / light / energy)
- Two colors do the work. High contrast, no gradients. (ZAO's wider palette is Blue #141e27 + Yellow #e0ddaa;
  the festival look runs the navy/gold above - reconcile later, but stay consistent within a piece.)

## Visual language (the look)
Navy + gold. Crowd silhouettes. Stage light. Hands up. Motion. A 50-agent Artifact ideation pass converged on
exactly this, so it is the signature look:
- Silhouettes over faces (universal, ageless, no language barrier).
- Gold light as the energy - sharp, pulsing, hard-edged.
- Real festival footage over stock. Movement over stills (GIF/short video wherever possible).
- Square 1:1 for Artifacts and avatars; never put text inside the image.

## Voice
Two registers, same brand:
- **Online / community:** casual, hype, countdown-driven. "zm" is the house greeting (our "gm"). Rally the
  room, count the days, celebrate the artists.
- **Local / Ellsworth:** lead with music, zero jargon, never mention web3 or crypto. "A free outdoor music
  festival, October 3, all day." Curiosity carries the rest.

## Logo / mark (to build)
- A primary "ZAO Festivals" wordmark + a small emblem that works tiny (X avatar, Artifact corner) to huge
  (stage). Modular. Until it exists, the wordmark in the brand colors is the mark.

## Do
- Use the ZAO-[name] system for every chapter.
- Keep navy + gold, high contrast, real footage, motion.
- Codify "zm" as the greeting; document each chapter as brand history (collect the posters).
- One signature Instagram-worthy moment on-site (free branding).

## Don't
- No text or graphic overlays inside an Artifact/hero image.
- No web3/crypto language to the local Ellsworth audience.
- No emojis or em dashes in ZAO copy (house rule). No autocorrecting the brand names.
- Don't drift the palette mid-piece.
